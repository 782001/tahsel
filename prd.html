Project Overview / Executive Summary** This project aims to enhance user experience within the FocusFlow mobile application by introducing customizable notification sounds and vibration patterns. Currently, FocusFlow relies on generic system defaults, leading to user complaints about lack of personalization and difficulty distinguishing FocusFlow alerts from other app notifications. By providing tailored alert options, we intend to increase user satisfaction, improve engagement, and reinforce FocusFlow's position as a personalized productivity tool. ### **2. Problem Statement** FocusFlow users frequently express a desire for more control over their in-app notifications. The current generic system default sounds and vibration patterns are often described as disruptive, indistinguishable from other apps, or simply not aligned with users' personal preferences or work environments. This leads to: * **User Frustration:** Lack of personalization reduces the perceived value of notifications. * **Missed Alerts:** Users may ignore or dismiss generic alerts, missing important task reminders. * **Distraction:** Inappropriate sounds can break focus rather than aid it. * **Competitive Disadvantage:** Many competitors offer some form of notification customization. ### **3. Goals & Objectives** * **Primary Goal:** Increase user satisfaction and engagement by providing personalized notification experiences. * **Specific Objectives (SMART):** * Achieve a 15% increase in positive feedback related to notification settings within 3 months post-launch. * Reduce notification-related customer support tickets by 20% within 6 months post-launch. * Increase the average session duration by 5% for users who customize their notifications. * Ensure feature adoption by at least 30% of daily active users (DAU) within 6 months post-launch. ### **4. Target Audience** All FocusFlow users, with a particular emphasis on: * **Power Users:** Those who extensively customize their app experiences. * **Users in Diverse Environments:** Individuals who need distinct alerts for focused work (e.g., quiet chime), or more prominent alerts in noisy environments. * **Users reliant on reminders:** Individuals who heavily depend on FocusFlow's task and deadline reminders. ### **5. Proposed Solution & Key Features** The solution involves adding a dedicated "Notification Settings" section within the app where users can customize alerts for different event types. **Key Features (MVP):** 1. **Dedicated Notification Settings Screen:** A new section under "Settings" for managing all notification preferences. 2. **Per-Notification Type Customization:** * Users can select specific event types (e.g., "Task Reminder," "Deadline Alert," "Daily Digest"). * For each type, they can: * Choose from a curated library of pre-set notification sounds (e.g., "Gentle Chime," "Productivity Pulse," "Sharp Alert," "Silent"). * Choose from a curated library of pre-set vibration patterns (e.g., "Short Pulse," "Long Buzz," "Rhythmic Tap," "None"). 3. **Preview Functionality:** Allow users to preview selected sounds and vibration patterns before saving. 4. **Reset to Default Option:** A clear option to revert all custom settings back to system defaults. ### **6.
